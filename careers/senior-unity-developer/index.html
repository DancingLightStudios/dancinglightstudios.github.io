About Us

Dancing Light Studios stands at the exciting intersection of education and entertainment technology. We’re a team of empathetic innovators, fueled by respect, adaptability, and humility. Our mission: reshaping the future of classrooms and venues worldwide through advanced, engaging, and beneficial tech solutions.

The Opportunity

At Dancing Light Studios, the opportunity for a Senior Unity Developer goes beyond just a job; it’s an exciting chance to pioneer and shape the future of entertainment technology. You’ll thrive in our unique cell-based team structure where collaboration with other passionate and driven individuals is key, shared objectives drive collective innovation, and individual contributions are celebrated. The experiences and products you’ll help develop will have a significant and meaningful impact on countless lives. Working on high-tech location-based entertainment projects, you’ll dive into a world of Augmented Reality (AR), Virtual Reality (VR), projection mapping, immersive displays, and mesmerizing soundscapes. Our aim is to make every workday an adventure, and you’ll play a pivotal role in that journey.

Responsibilities

  • Design and implement well-engineered, reliable, and maintainable code.
  • Collaborate with team members to develop various gameplay systems.
  • Creative innovative new solutions in the world of location-based entertainment.
  • The ability to both prototype new ideas quickly and polish validated ideas to perfection.
  • Test code, fix bugs, and profile and optimize code.
  • Document and peer review technical designs with other software developers.
  • Work with QA, providing the tools and resources needed to ensure our products hit the highest standards of quality.
  • Monitor, mentor, and direct developers under your technical supervision to maximize their potential, their efficiency, and their adherence to the studio’s code quality and standards.

Requirements

  • Minimum 5 years of industry experience as a Software Developer or similar role.
  • Proven experience with Unity or Unreal.
  • At least one shipped title.
  • Extensive knowledge of C# and/or C++.
  • Experience with gameplay programming and tool development.
  • Strong understanding of 3D math and computer graphics.
  • A degree in Computer Science, related field, or equivalent work experience.
  • Excellent problem-solving and communication skills.
  • A creative thinker who can bring new ideas to the table.

Why Join Dancing Light Studios

At Dancing Light Studios, we value authenticity, collaboration, and the power of innovation. When you join our team, you are not just accepting a role; you are embracing a community of empathetic innovators, bound by a shared mission and core values. We are a team where everyone’s voice is respected and every opinion is valued. With us, you are more than a Software Developer; you’re a key player in shaping the future of immersive technologies in education and entertainment. Our workspace thrives on mutual respect and continuous learning - an environment where making mistakes is accepted, because we’re all human and we learn from them. We’re transparent, honest, and direct but also professional, respectful, and welcoming. If you are looking for a team where your contributions matter, your growth is encouraged, and your individuality is celebrated, Dancing Light Studios could be your next great adventure.
